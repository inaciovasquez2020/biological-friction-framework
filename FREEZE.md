# FREEZE POLICY

## Canonical Surface

The following files define the public claim boundary:

- README.md
- STATUS.md
- FREEZE.md
- CITATION.cff

## Supporting Surface

Non-canonical unless explicitly promoted:

- scripts/
- workflows/
- notes/
- data/
- examples/
- generated artifacts/

## Mutation Policy

- main tracks canonical state.
- edits must preserve repository identity and scope consistency.
